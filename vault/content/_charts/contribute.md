## Ways to Contribute

- Share chart ideas, corrections, or feedback on [Discord]({{ site.discord_url }}).
- Send chart files and updates by email: `dhammacharts@protonmail.com`.
- Share references and source links for existing chart pages.

## What Helps Most

- Clear source citations for doctrinal claims.
- Image assets with readable text and source files when possible.
- Notes about language/version updates for existing charts.
